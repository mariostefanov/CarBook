About us

Welcome to Carbook

CarBook is a peer-to-peer car sharing platform that offers a sustainable and affordable way to travel around Bulgaria. Our mission is to connect car owners with people who need a ride, making transportation more efficient and eco-friendly.

We believe that car sharing is the future of transportation, and we strive to create a community of responsible drivers who care about the environment and want to make a positive impact on society. With CarBook, you can find a car for your next trip, save money on transportation, and contribute to a more sustainable future.
